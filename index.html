Overview

#techLA is a city-wide initiative to engage the technology community in civic innovation, featuring monthly events, a technology challenge, and many opportunities to strengthen L.A.

 

 

Challenge:LA

#techLA is hosting a summer-long competition that challenges Angelenos to address some of the cityâ€™s most pressing issues. It's time to take advantage of citizen-generated solutions. We invite all Los Angeles citizens - technologists, designers, data scientists, public servants, and other community members - to participate in these events and find solutions around three areas of public need:


Transportation

  • Optimize citizen engagement around parking availability and signage
  • Build multi-modal (e.g. bike + car share) applications for transportation coordination
  • Reduce pedestrian and bike injuries


Community

  • Support enrollment in new immigration reform programs (DAPA/DACA)
  • Facilitate citizen engagement in public affairs via neighborhood council participation
  • Streamline processes for launching local small businesses


Save the Drop

  • Encourage individual citizens and local businesses to reduce water usage
  • Analyze public infrastructure to optimize water management practices
  • Explore both hardware and software approaches to water conservation

Challenge:LA Prizes

Challenge:LA winners will be invited to participate in Accelerate:LA, a civic technology accelerator program run by our partners at the Civic Innovation Lab. The program is designed to encourage the sustainability of each solution and ensure its real-world social impact.
